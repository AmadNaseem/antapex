Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

The short version. AntapexAI is an offline, on-device AI assistant. Your conversations are generated and stored entirely on your device. We run no servers, we have no accounts, and we do not collect, transmit, sell, or track any of your personal data or chats.

1. Who we are

AntapexAI ("the app", "we", "us") is a private, on-device artificial-intelligence assistant. After a one-time model download, the app runs a language model locally on your device and works with no internet connection. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

2. Data processed on your device

The following stays on your device and is never sent to us or any third party:

  • Conversations: your prompts and the AI's replies are processed by the on-device model and saved in the app's private local storage.
  • Local profile: an optional display name and email you may enter are stored only on your device to label your data. No verification, no server account.
  • Settings & PIN: your preferences and an optional app-lock PIN are stored locally; the PIN is kept only as a one-way hash.

We have no ability to access any of this. It never leaves the device.

3. Internet access

The app uses the internet only for these purposes:

  • Model download: the first time you set it up, the app downloads an open-source AI model file from a public model host (e.g. Hugging Face). This is a normal file download and does not include your data.
  • App updates: handled by your app store, not by us.

Once the model is installed, chatting requires no internet at all.

4. Permissions

  • Internet — to download the model as described above.
  • Microphone (optional): only if you use voice dictation. Speech is converted to text by your device's own speech-recognition service. Depending on your device and its settings, that OS-level service may process audio using its provider (for example, Google on Android); this is governed by your device maker's privacy policy, not by us. If you never use voice input, no audio is accessed.

5. What we do NOT do

  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
  • No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party trackers or social SDKs.
  • No selling or sharing of any data — because we never receive any.

6. Your control & data deletion

  • You can delete individual conversations, or clear all chats, at any time in the app.
  • You can delete the downloaded model to free space.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all app data from your device.

7. Children

AntapexAI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone — because the app collects no data at all.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at this page.
